Ideas
Curated picks by category. Not a checklist—grab what fits the mood.
🥾 Active
Lanikai Pillbox Hike at sunrise
Best hike-to-view ratio on the island. Sunrise version is uncrowded, light on the Mokuluas is a different experience.
Already in Day 3
Hanauma Bay snorkeling
Protected marine reserve inside a volcanic crater. Reef in knee-deep water. Book the early timed slot.
Koko Head Crater Stairs
1,048 railroad-tie steps straight up. The serious hike. Fully exposed, go at sunrise. Pairs with Hanauma Bay morning.
Kayak to Mokulua Islands
~30 min paddle from Lanikai to a protected cove on Moku Nui. Most tourists stop at the beach.
Pairs with Pillbox morning
Secret Island at Kualoa Ranch
Private beach via their tours. Ko'olau mountain wall backdrop. Jurassic Park filming location.
Already in Day 4
🌺 Relaxing
House Without a Key at sunset
Open-air, live steel guitar, hula dancer, Mai Tai. Next door to our hotel. The anti-luau.
Already in Day 5
Ala Moana Beach Park
Where locals go. Calm protected water, wide beach, no tourists. 10 min from hotel. Bring takeout poke.
Spa day at Halepuna or Halekulani
Use the $100 Amex credit at the pool. Check if Halekulani next door offers spa access.
Early morning Waikiki walk
Before 7am. Nearly empty, soft light, still water. Free.
Honolulu Museum of Art
1920s Mediterranean building, open-air courtyards. Monet, Picasso, Hokusai, strong Asian art. Air-conditioned.
Day 5 option
🎲 Wildcard
Outrigger canoe ride
Paddle out, catch waves coming in off Waikiki. Traditional Hawaiian water sport. 30-45 min, more intimate than a catamaran.
Yoga class at a local studio
Yoga Hawaii or Still & Moving Center for drop-ins. Practicing somewhere new.
Makapu'u Lighthouse Trail at sunrise
Paved 2-mile clifftop walk. Nearly alone at dawn. Open Pacific + Rabbit Island views. Stacks into a windward coast morning.
Chinatown evening cocktail crawl
Bar Leather Apron (one of the best cocktail bars in the US), Tchin Tchin, Smith & Kings. Pair with dim sum or pho.
Ka'ena Point
5-mile flat coastal trail to the westernmost tip of Oahu. Monk seals, albatross, stark landscape. Half-day. The "we walked to the edge" story.
