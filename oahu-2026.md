## Itinerary

> Items marked with a checkmark are confirmed. Everything else is a suggestion -- still being refined.

### Day 1 -- Sat Jun 13: Arrive + Senia

- Arrive HNL 11:08am (AS 821) &#10003;
- Check into Halepuna Waikiki by Halekulani
- Settle in, walk Waikiki Beach
- Sunset from the hotel or beach
- **Dinner: Senia** -- Chef's Counter, 6:30pm &#10003;

### Day 2 -- Sun Jun 14: Diamond Head + Pearl Harbor

- **6:00am -- Diamond Head Crater Hike** -- 1.8 miles, 560ft gain, moderate. Go at opening to beat crowds and heat.
- **Leonard's Bakery** on the way back -- malasadas (Portuguese donuts). Cash only.
- **Pearl Harbor / USS Arizona Memorial** -- book timed entry in advance (free, fills up fast). Late-morning/early-afternoon slot pairs well with Diamond Head morning.
- Back to hotel, decompress
- **Dinner: Mud Hen Water** -- Ed Kenney, farm-to-table Hawaiian. Relaxed vibe.

### Day 3 -- Mon Jun 15: Windward Coast

- Drive **Pali Highway** -- stop at **Nu'uanu Pali Lookout** (dramatic mountain views)
- **Lanikai Pillbox Hike** -- 1.8 miles, moderate scramble. Views of Lanikai and the Mokulua Islands from above.
- **Lanikai Beach** -- small, protected, incredible color. Mokulua Island views.
- **Kailua Beach** -- white sand, turquoise water, more space. 10 min from Lanikai.
- **Kalapawai Market** in Kailua for lunch -- local sandwiches, un-touristy
- Return to Waikiki
- **Dinner:** Casual -- hotel restaurant, poke, or Waikiki neighborhood pick

### Day 4 -- Tue Jun 16: North Shore Loop

- Drive up the **Windward coast** (more scenic than H-1)
- **Kualoa Ranch** -- Jurassic Park valley. ATV or horseback optional.
  - **Secret Island** -- private beach via their tours. Dramatic backdrop, very photogenic. Most tourists don't know it exists.
- **Giovanni's Shrimp Truck** (Kahuku) for lunch -- North Shore institution
- **Haleiwa town** -- surf culture hub, shops
- **Matsumoto's Shave Ice** -- since 1951
- Return via H-2 or continue the coastal loop
- **Dinner:** Casual Waikiki -- back late, tired from driving

### Day 5 -- Wed Jun 17: Slow Day -- Pool, Beach, Waikiki

- Sleep in. Hotel pool. Use the $100 Amex property credit.
- Late morning: Waikiki Beach or **Ala Moana Beach Park** (local favorite, less crowded)
- Afternoon: optional **Honolulu Museum of Art** or keep it fully relaxed
- **Sunset cocktails: House Without a Key at Halekulani** -- open air, live Hawaiian music, hula dancer. Next door to our hotel. The iconic Oahu sundowner.
- **Dinner: Vintage Cave** (if appetite for the splurge) -- 12-course tasting, cave-like atmosphere. Or skip if Senia was enough.

### Day 6 -- Thu Jun 18: Chinatown + Flex

- Morning: **Chinatown Honolulu** -- walkable, art galleries, local food stalls
- Afternoon: Flexible -- revisit a beach, catch something you missed, or stay in Waikiki
- **Final nice dinner** -- dealer's choice. Vintage Cave if skipped Day 5, Mud Hen Water repeat, or something new.

### Day 7 -- Fri Jun 19: Last Full Day

- Morning: Sleep in or one last beach session
- **Lunch:** Last real Oahu meal -- make it count
- Afternoon: Pack, walk Waikiki, souvenirs
- **Dinner:** Easy -- casual spot, hotel, or early and light

### Day 8 -- Sat Jun 20: Depart

- Depart HNL 11:05am (AS 839) &#10003;
- Arrive Seattle 7:54pm
- Leave hotel by ~8:30am. Request late checkout but don't count on it.
